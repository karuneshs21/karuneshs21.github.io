Voting Rules for Thee

Please give each participant's slime a score from 0 to 10 for each category

Review your votes carefully before you hit submit. If you make a mistake, contact the owner of the website. Your wrong vote will have to be manually erased. This is allowed only at the discretion of the owner so don't rely on it!
Someone getting access to your password will not get you a new password. Don't share your password with anyone!!
Fraudulent/Joke submissions will need to be manually removed one by one. The more there are, the longer the vote counting will take. If there are too many of them, this voting website will be shut down for everyone
